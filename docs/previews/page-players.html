The standard

What we look for.

Three filters. They run before any conversation. Anything that doesn't pass stays as a watched profile until it does.

01

Skill & potential.

Raw ability on the ball, tactical awareness, and the physical tools to compete at the next level. Evaluated across all positions.

02

Drive & dedication.

Natural talent is the starting point. What separates professionals is the commitment to improving every single day, on and off the pitch.

03

Character & coachability.

Professional clubs look at the whole player. We represent athletes with the mindset to accept feedback, grow, and lead by example.
