Our story

Built from the ground up.

Mission

ProKickTalent represents serious U17–U23 Canadian footballers — independent of any club or academy, aligned to one outcome: the right next move for the player.

Representation starts with evaluation. The player file is built before any introduction is made.

Families navigating academy exposure, college, semi-pro, CPL, and European trials deserve clear guidance grounded in scouting, GPS data, and FIFA-aligned representation standards. That clarity — not a quick introduction — is the product.

The path

No shortcuts.

2018

Player advisory begins.

Informally advising Ontario players and families on academy exposure, trial decisions, and the red flags families miss.

2021

FIFA-aligned representation.

Representation built around FIFA standards — contract awareness, advocacy through trials and offers, professional independence.

2024

ProKickTalent Sports Group founded.

Formalized as a dedicated agency: scouting, GPS, and advisory under one practice, based in Toronto, serving Ontario and Canada.

2026

GPS analytics integrated.

Every active player file now pairs match scouting with GPS performance reporting — distance, sprint output, intensity, workload.
