What changed from v1

Less, but better.

v1 — generic
  • Bebas Neue everywhere
  • 4 accents (gold/green/electric/warn)
  • Component-grid hero w/ overlays
  • 7+ sections per page
  • Glass cards + heavy grain
  • Tactic board, GPS readout, scout cards in the hero
  • Bento everything
v2 — editorial
  • Fraunces (serif) + Inter, italic emphasis
  • 1 accent (gold) + 1 data tone, monochrome otherwise
  • Editorial-statement hero, photo + line
  • 5 sections, generous whitespace
  • Flat surfaces + hairlines, faint grain
  • Hero is just the moment; data lives in the body
  • Editorial lists + quiet columns

Decided

Light default · dark on toggle.

Light / warm paper is now the default (the Players' Tribune / Gestifute / Mundial model) — the bigger differentiator from your old dark brand. The ◐ light button top-right flips to the dark editorial variant (Wasserman model) and remembers your choice. Try both; the site ships light.
