A1.b · Typography directions

Pick the voice.

Four directions distilled from peer research (full table in docs/plans/2026-05-23-peer-research.md). Each direction renders the same content so you can compare the personality of the typography, not the layout.

All four use free Google Fonts — production-ready. The current Bebas+Inter pairing is shown first as the baseline to compare against.

00 · Baseline (current)

Bebas Neue + Inter

Bebas Neue (display) · Inter (body)

Where you've seen it: Bebas is the default condensed display sans of the past decade. Used everywhere from bootcamp portfolios to startup landing pages. Free, recognizable, hard to make feel premium.

01 · Editorial-football · recommended

Fraunces + Inter

Fraunces variable serif (display · italic emphasis) · Inter (body · UI)

Reference: The Players' Tribune (Publico + Graphik). Fraunces is a free, variable serif that approximates Publico's wedge-serif character. The editorial-italic move is what gives Wasserman/THE·TEAM and Players' Tribune their "authored" feel rather than "marketed."

02 · Sport-grotesque

Anton + Public Sans

Anton (heavy condensed display) · Public Sans (variable body)

Reference: Mundial Magazine (Sant'Elia Sans), Copa90 (Oswald + Diatype), Formula 1 (custom "Formula1 Wide"). Anton is the free Google Fonts cousin of those condensed display families. Public Sans is the IBM/USWDS humanist sans — same family as Mundial.

03 · Quiet luxury

Cormorant Garamond + Inter

Cormorant Garamond (classical serif display · italic) · Inter (body)

Reference: the Loro Piana / Moncler / quiet-money family. Cormorant Garamond is a free Garamond revival with elegant italic. Most overtly "luxurious" of the four — leans away from sport, toward an old-money advisory practice.

04 · Modern agency

Manrope (single family)

Manrope (display + body + UI) — one geometric sans across all roles

Reference: Wasserman/THE·TEAM (Gotham), Stellar Entertainment (Avenir Next), Roc Nation Sports (Roboto Condensed). Single-family humanist/geometric sans is the "modern agency" baseline. Manrope is the free, well-cut equivalent of Gotham/Avenir.

Recommendation

01 · Editorial-football (Fraunces + Inter)

Closest to the brief ("luxurious, inviting, professional"). The italic gives the brand emotion without ornament. Closest peer in the research (Players' Tribune) is the most successful athlete-centric premium site in the set.

If you want more sport energy: 02 Sport-grotesque. If you want maximum quiet luxury and don't mind moving away from sport: 03 Quiet luxury. If safer / more familiar is the goal: 04 Modern agency.
