Our story

About
ProKickTalent.

Mission

Built for Canadian youth football.

ProKickTalent represents serious U17–U23 Canadian footballers. Independent of any club or academy. Aligned to one outcome: the right next move for the player.

Representation starts with evaluation. The player file is built before any introduction is made.

The agency exists because families navigating academy exposure, college, semi-pro, CPL, and European trials deserve clear guidance grounded in scouting, GPS data, and FIFA-aligned representation standards.

Path

From the ground up.

No shortcuts. Each chapter of the agency is anchored to evidence and decisions made for actual players.

2018

Player advisory begins.

Started informally advising Ontario players and families on academy exposure, trial decisions, and family-side red flags.

2021

FIFA-aligned representation.

Built representation practice around FIFA standards: contract awareness, advocacy through trials and offers, professional independence.

2024

ProKickTalent Sports Group founded.

Formalized as a dedicated agency with scouting + GPS + advisory under one practice. Based in Toronto, serving Ontario and broader Canada.

2026

GPS analytics integrated into evaluations.

Every active player file pairs match scouting with GPS performance reporting — distance, sprint output, intensity, workload.
